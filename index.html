My Projects

These are the projects that I've worked on at App Academy and on my own. I hope to keep updating them and add more in the future.

WeView

WeView is a video hosting and streaming platform with a focus on education. Teachers can upload videos, and students can watch them anywhere, anytime. While they watch, students can leave comments, but unlike YouTube, where comments appear at the bottom of the video, WeView comments are timestamped, and by default will appear at the same time in the video that they were made.

Check out Weview

Wavesense

Do you wish there was a way to vibe with audio visually and audibly rather than only listening to it? WaveSense is an audio visualizer application that enables users to select songs or upload local audio files, then feeds the selected audio into a visual display reflective of the audio waves.

Listen and watch on WaveSense
